Dishoom's Chicken Biryani

A mixed dish of riceh and chicken from the Indian subcontinent. This particular one uses a wide variety of spices and chicken to bring michilen star cooking to your home.

For the rice:

  • 300g white basmati rice
  • 2 tsp fine sea salt
  • Juice 1/2 lime

  • 500g skinless, bonless free-range chicken thighs each cut into 3 pieces
  • 20g ginger paste
  • 25 garlic paste
  • 1 1/2 tsp deggi mirch
  • 2 tsp fine sea salt
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 100g full-fat greek yogurt
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 3 green chillies, each cut into 4 long strips
  • 3cm fresh root ginger, cut into fine matchsticks
  • 100g crispy fried onions (widely avaiable)
  • 6 large fresh mint leaves, chopped
  • 5g fresh coriander leaves, chopped
