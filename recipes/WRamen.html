Wagamamas Shirodashi Pork Ramen

Back home

Slow cooked pork belly marinated in bulgogi sauce served with noodles in a rich chicken broth with dashi and miso. topped with seasonal greens, scallions, wakame, menma and half a tea-stained egg

Ingredients:

  • 700 ml of chicken stock
  • 5 tablespoons of soy sauce (1 tbsp for seasoning)
  • 3 cloves of garlic, halved
  • 1 sliced ginger
  • 1 tps of Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 tps of Chinese five-spice
  • 375 grams of ramen noodles
  • 2 tps of sesame oil
  • 400 grams of cocked pork
  • 1 tps of white sugar, optional
  • A pinch of chili powder
    •

    For the garnishing:

  • 4 eggs, boiled, peeled and halved
  • 100 grams of baby spinach
  • 1 sheet of nori, dried and shredded
  • Spring onions sliced
    •

    How to cook:

    1. First prepare by halving the garlic and slicing the ginger.
    2. Mix the chicken stock, 4 tablespoons of soy sauce, garlic cloves, sliced ginger, Worcestershire sauce, chili powder, Chinese five-spice, and 300 ml of water in a large saucepan or stockpot.
    3. Now boil your mixture over high heat.
    4. Bring down the heat and let it simmer for 5 minutes.
    5. Taste your stock now. Add some more soy sauce or 1 teaspoon of white sugar if you want to make it saltier or sweeter.
    6. Cook your ramen noodle according to the package instructions.
    7. Drain it and keep aside.
    8. Now slice the cooked chicken or pork.
    9. Fry it in 2 teaspoons of sesame oil until you see it beginning to brown. Set aside.
    10. You are almost done. Divide your noodles between 4 bowls.
    11. Top them all with 25% of the meat, half an egg for each bowl (2 boiled eggs divided between the 4 bowls), and 25 grams of spinach.
    12. Now drain your stock into a pan. Boil once more.
    13. Sprinkle 1 shredded nori sheet, sesame seeds, and sliced shallots or spring onions.
    14. Let the spinach wait for a while before serving.
