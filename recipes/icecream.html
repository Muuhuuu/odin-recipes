Homemade Ice cream

Back home

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of heavy whipping cream
  • 2 cups half-and-half cream
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

How to cook:

  1. Combine all ingredients
  2. Stir until sugar is completely dissolved
  3. Fil a cylinder of ice cream maker (or a bag of ice) no more than 2/3 full
  4. Freeze until manufactures directions (for a bag until preferences)
  5. Serve immediately
